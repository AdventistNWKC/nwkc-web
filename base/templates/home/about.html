About North West kenya Conference

History

North west Kenya confernce (NWKC), was established in the year 2012 as a pilot conference. it was before, part of the greater western Kenya Conference whose head quaters was based in Eldoret town. NWKC is based in Webuye off the Webuye-Kitale highway.
